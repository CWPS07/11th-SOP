Raman) Dr. Chandrasekhara Venkata Raman (C.V Raman) Early ...Sir Chandrasekhara Venkata Raman was an iconic Indian physicist renowned for his 1928 discovery of the Raman Effect, a phenomenon of light scattering that earned him the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1930. His pioneering research in optics and acoustics also explored why seawater appears blue. A pioneer in Indian science, he was the first non-white person to win a Nobel Prize and was honored with the Bharat Ratna in 1954, India's highest civilian award.
